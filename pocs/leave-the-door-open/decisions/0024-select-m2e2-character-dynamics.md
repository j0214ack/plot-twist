# 0024: Select the M2E2 Character Dynamic

Status: Accepted

Date: 2026-07-16

## Context

The current accepted Character Cores distinguish the spouses mainly as a
systems-oriented husband and a spatially attentive wife. The resulting runtime
voices can still converge into the same careful, resistant cadence.

`characters/couple-auditions-3x3.md` tested three Martin directions against
three Elise directions before and after the yellow-bowl event. Job-blind
finalists were then compared in an ordinary rain-and-soup scene, where there was
no dramatic event to help the profiles advertise themselves. M2E2 produced the
most credible low-volume domestic difference without requiring either person to
state the character design.

## Decision

Use M2E2 as the authored relationship and performance direction.

- **Martin M2 — social narrator:** Martin remembers people, details, turns of
  phrase, and shared stories. He maintains contact by listening, asking, and
  keeping a small joke alive. Under strain his public fluency can disappear at
  home, and questions or explanations can become a way to resist helplessness.
- **Elise E2 — spatial and patient:** Elise notices placement, timing, routes,
  and the instant before a movement becomes a decision. She cares by making
  room and remaining present. Under strain she delays, then makes one concrete
  adjustment or one precise request. Her perception is observational, not
  mystical.

The auditioned occupations, professional vocabulary, hobbies, and exact
biographical decorations are not accepted by this decision. They remain
authoring candidates. M2E2 names a relational engine, attention pattern, and
acting difference—not a requirement that every line perform a trait.

Ordinary scenes remain ordinary. Martin need not ask multiple questions in
every exchange, and Elise need not manage the placement of every object. The
profiles should be recognizable over several beats rather than through a
catchphrase in each reply.

## Requirements

- **LDO-CHAR-M2E2-001 — Distinction over display:** multiple beats should reveal
  Martin's contact-through-language and Elise's contact-through-placement or
  timing, but an isolated line may be neutral.
- **LDO-CHAR-M2E2-002 — No profession leakage:** a profession is not used as a
  voice shortcut until separately authored and accepted.
- **LDO-CHAR-M2E2-003 — Grief transforms existing behavior:** later restraint
  should be a changed form of earlier social or spatial care, not a replacement
  personality invented only to serve the puzzle.
- **LDO-CHAR-M2E2-004 — No caricature:** Martin's questions do not make him a
  therapist or debater in every scene; Elise's spatial attention does not grant
  intuition about facts or motives she did not observe.

## Consequences

`characters/husband.md`, `characters/wife.md`, and the runtime Character Core
now express the selected M2E2 direction through a traced TDD cycle. Chapter
MindState still owns scene-specific resistance; M2E2 does not replace the
authored psychological graph.

The full 3x3 audition remains exploratory evidence. M2E2 is the selected row,
while its specific sample dialogue and occupations are not automatically
canonical.
